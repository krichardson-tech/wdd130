Featured Climbing Destinations

Smith Rock

The birthplace of American sport climbing, located in Terrebonne, Oregon. It has over 1000 routes climbing on welded tuff. The “Monkey Face” pictured above is a 350ft pillar boasting Oregon’s 2nd hardest route, Just Do It.

World Wall

The World Wall is one of the best sport faces on the planet. It’s located on Mt Si in North Bend, WA, and gives hundreds of feet of crimpy sport climbing on really smooth rock. You can regularly find some of the best climbers in the country there.

Flagstone

Flagstone is Oregon’s single best slab climbing destination. The majority of the routes here need a ridiculous amount of technique and footwork. The East Wall here boasts some of the best overhung terrain in the PNW!
